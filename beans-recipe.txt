Ingredients:
 - 3 cups of beans
 - 1 small onion
 - 2 teaspoon salt
 - 1/2 cup red oil
 - 2 small red pepper
 - 7 cups of water
 - 3 spoons of crayfish

Directions:
1. Cook the beans for 30mins.

2. Cut onions and pepper. After 30mins, add salk, onions and pepper and cook for 10 mins.

3. Add crayfish and red oil and cook for 7 mins. If you have plantain, you can chot it in sugar cubes and add with the crayfish and cook for 10mins. 

4. Serve while hot.
